Privacy Policy

App: Crazy Discgolf

Last updated: 2026-06-10

Overview

Crazy Discgolf is a game app. The app does not require account creation and does not ask users to directly provide personal profile information.

Data the app may process

  • Gameplay and settings data stored locally on your device for app functionality.
  • Advertising-related data may be processed by the ad provider (Google Mobile Ads), such as device identifiers, diagnostics, and approximate location data, according to Google policies.
  • Basic technical diagnostics (for ad delivery and stability) through third-party SDKs.

Advertising

Crazy Discgolf uses Google Mobile Ads to show ads. Ad delivery and related data use are governed by Google's privacy terms: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

How data is used

  • To run core gameplay features.
  • To save and restore local game progress.
  • To display ads and maintain ad service quality.

Data sharing

The app does not sell personal data. Limited data may be processed by third-party ad providers as needed for ad delivery and fraud prevention.

Children's privacy

Crazy Discgolf is not directed specifically to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data inappropriately, contact support to request review.

Your choices

  • You can stop app data processing by uninstalling the app.
  • You can manage ad/privacy controls via your device settings where available.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at https://github.com/fooaldrin/crazydiscgolf/issues.

This page is provided for App Store compliance and user transparency. Update it whenever data practices or SDK usage changes.
